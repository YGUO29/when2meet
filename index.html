Meeting time finder

Pick a city for each participant. The tool checks every hour in the next 24 hours, drops anything inside someone's sleep window, and ranks the rest by how many people are inside business hours.

Participants

Add 2 or more people. Email is optional — if filled, they're auto-invited when you select a time. Your email is saved to your browser only (not public).

Meeting settings

All times are interpreted in each participant's local timezone.
